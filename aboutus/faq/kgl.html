KGL International language competition

1. What is the international KGL language competition?

KGL competition is one of the most popular English language competitions in the world. The main goal of these competitions is to encourage students to learn English and improve their general knowledge of the language.

2. Which country is the main committee of the KGL competition?

The KGL competition is held in England under the supervision of the International Testing Organization.

3. KGL competitions are being held in Iran since what year?

So far, 70 countries from all over the world have joined the KGL international community, and Erdos Institute has been organizing these competitions in the country since 2020 as the exclusive representative of Iran.

4. Who can participate in this competition?

All students between the ages of 7 and 17 can participate in these competitions. These competitions are held in 5 levels.

5. When is the date of the competition?

The first stage of this competition is held every year at the end of February. The second stage is at the end of April and the third stage is in June.

6. What resources should be studied to prepare for these competitions?

KGL topics are determined based on the common language books of European countries (CEFR) and are offered from PREA1 to B2 level. The purpose of these competitions is to evaluate the general language of students, and therefore, all general language teaching books can be used as a suitable source. However, since the organizer of this competition is England, the listening and speaking part will be in British accent.

7. What is the ranking of KGL competition questions?

The questions of the competition are designed in 5 levels PREA1 to B2, which usually covers the level of Elementary to Advanced.

8. How can I get a sample of questions from previous years of KGL competitions?

You can download the sample questions of the 2020 first stage competitions from the erdos.ir site in the KGL competitions section. Also, the sample book of past years' questions is available on our website, in the store section.

9. What are the benefits of participating in the KGL competition?

1. Students aged 7 to 17 can participate in KGL competitions. KGL is a high-standard fun competition to motivate students around the world to better learn English as a second language.

2. KGL exams have the highest quality and international standards and can be a suitable standard for preparing students for the assessments they will need in the future.داشت .

3. All KGL participants will be given the necessary information for the selection criteria for the second stage. The best people of each grade in each stage will receive a certificate of participation in the competition, and also the finalists will be invited to participate in the final competition in June 2022 in Europe, and all expenses for three-day stay and participation in the final stage of the competition will be covered by the scientific committee. KGL is provided.

4. One of the great advantages of these competitions is the low cost of participation, in that students only pay for the first stage and participation in the second stage and the final stage of the competition is free.

10. What are the age requirements to participate in the 2021 competition?

To participate in the contest, KGL participants must meet the following age requirements:

a) All those born from December 2009 to December 2014 and with a maximum age of 11 years can participate in PREA1 level.
b) All those born from December 2008 to December 2009 with a maximum age of 12 years can participate in A1 level.
c) All those born from December 2006 to December 2008 with a maximum age of 14 years can participate in A2 level.
d) All those born from December 2005 to December 2006 and with a maximum age of 15 years can participate in B1 level.
e) All those born from December 2003 to December 2005 and with a maximum age of 17 years can participate in B2 level.

 

Participants should determine their level according to their educational level and age and participate in the competition. For example, student A, who is currently studying in the fifth grade and is 12 years old, must participate in Level A1. If a student this year participates in the first stage at A1 level and makes it to the second stage, he must register for competitions at a higher level, i.e. A2, next year. But if he could not be accepted in the first stage this year, he can register again at the same level next year.

11. Introduce the official site of KGL competitions?

The official site of the competition is http://www.kglcontest.org.

12. What is the number of questions in the KGL competition and how to answer them?

The number of questions for PRE A1 level is equal to 35 questions and the answer time is 45 minutes

The number of questions for level A1-B2 is equal to 40 questions and the answer time is 60 minutes

13. Do the questions have a negative score?

No, the questions do not have negative marks.

14. How many stages are the KGL competitions and where are they held?

This competition has 3 stages, the first and second stages are in Iran and the final stage is in a European country. Due to the spread of Corona, the final stage is currently held online in Iran.

15. What skills are asked at each stage?

The first stage of the competition includes Listening and Reading questions. In the second stage, writing questions are also added to the competition questions. Of course, the important point is that in the second stage, the listening and reading section of each candidate is checked first, and if you get a sufficient score in this section, then your writing is checked and your total score is announced, which determines which students find their way to the final stage. The third stage of the competition will include all four skills of Listening, Reading, Writing and Speaking.

16. How will the results of the competition be evaluated and announced?

The results of the first stage will be announced about 20 days after the competition. For every 250 participants from Iran, the KGL competition committee selects a number for the second stage and finally the world stage, which number will be determined when the results are announced. But the important point is that for every 250 participants from Iran, one person will definitely go to the world stage. PREA level 1 students do not have a global stage, and every year the three selected people of the second stage at this level are awarded a gift by Erdos Institute.

17. How will the results be announced?

The results of each stage are announced on the official website of Erdos Institute. According to the main committee of the competition, only the names of the first, second, third and top participants in each level will be announced in the first and second stages, and no other information will be given.

18. Who will receive gifts from Erdos Institute and the competition committee?

All participants of the first stage will receive a plaque and a certificate of participation in the competition.

All the participants of the second stage will receive a plaque and a certificate of participation in the competition. Also, the 1st to 3rd placers of each level will be given precious medals and prizes by Erdos Institute.

In addition to plaques and prizes, the selected people for the third stage will also receive the appreciation cup of Erdos Institute.

The 1st to 3rd placers of the final stage will receive a medal, a plaque and a dollar prize from the competition committee.

Also, every year, one person who gets the best result in all competitions from the first to the third stage. A scholarship for English schools and a prize of 1000 dollars will be awarded.

19. When and how will the preparation classes for AMO competitions be held?

For more information, refer to the Erdos Institute’s Courses, Classes and Workshops page in the FAQ section.

20. How much is the registration fee for KGL tournaments?

The registration fee for the first stage is about 15 euros, which is announced every year in December, depending on the price of the euro. The second and third stages of the competition are free.

21. How can we find out about the validity of these competitions?

The high statistical population of the participants shows the validity of this test at the world level and the board and certificates of this test can be used as a valid resume for students.
