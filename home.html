The best services of Erdos Institute

As the first innovative educational center located in the growth and technology centers of the country's universities, Erdos Institute has been providing leading services in the field of education for students at different levels of education.
The main vision of the institute is to improve the scientific level of students based on creative thinking and better understanding of scientific concepts in the field of mathematics and science.
With the aim of "educational innovations", in addition to international activities and communication with foreign institutions, this institution has also included various services in its scope of activities.

Virtual education system

Participating in the online courses of Erdos Institute can be a right choice for people who want to They are looking for classes with a small number of students, high quality education and continuous support.

Holding international Olympiads

In continuation of its educational innovations, since 2017, Erdos Institute has been holding international Olympiads in mathematics, science, creativity and English language... as an exclusive representative in the whole country.

Holding internal tests

The test is a serious benchmark for people who have a specific goal and know what they want. With more than 5 years of brilliant experience in this field, Erdos Institute has helped to improve the academic level of students.

International camps

So far, more than 100 selected Iranian students have participated in the Kangaroo International Camp, which is held every summer in Turkey. Students from Italy and Bulgaria also participate in this camp.

Erdos will answer your questions...

1.Beginning of the activities of the Erdos Institute:

Erdoush Innovator Institute was established in 2016 with the aim of creating educational innovations in the field of mathematics and sciences in the innovation center of Tabriz University. This institute has been established by Dr. Afshin Bahmaram of the faculty of the Faculty of Mathematics at the University of Tabriz and head of the team of the North West Student Olympiad of the country. The beginning of the activities of the Erdoush Institute has been held by holding public and private classes in Tehran and Tabriz as well as holding short workshops for gifted schools throughout the country.

2. List of educational activities of Erdos Institute?

  1. Holding in-person and online classes across the country
  2. Design and production of mathematical training equipment
  3. Compilation and translation of educational books
  4. Holding domestic and international competitions as the exclusive representative of the country throughout Iran
  5. Holding domestic and international educational tours
  6. Holding domestic and international workshops and conferences
  7. Providing consulting and talent services (New)

3.Vision and long-term goals of the Erdos Institute?

The main vision of the Institute is to develop young professionals who are able to provide combined knowledge in the fields of mathematics and science, computer science and language and can create innovative ideas in the above fields.‌The new world is the one who is in need of the world today. In this regard, promoting the scientific level of students based on creative thinking and better understanding of scientific concepts in the field of mathematics and science, etc. The main priority is Erdos.

4. Educational calendar of Erdos Institute's classes?

Thermic classes of the Erdos Institute cover four semesters in which the first three semesters (The first semester of summer, the second autumn semester and the third winter semester) One third of the subjects are taught each and the spring semester is intended to sum up the lessons and solve more problems.
Topics All classes are advanced level and to prepare students to participate in various scientific Olympiad.

5. Special classes for sixth grade students and gifted readiness?

Since 2018, Erdoush has been organizing full courses of gifted readiness (based on intelligences) and has a special program for gifted applicants that includes classes, tests and roundup courses.
The high acceptance rate in recent years is proof of the quality of our education.

6.Classroom support services?

The educational support of the institute monitors how to attend classes and do homework during the semester. It also bridges the interface between students and management on how classes are held. All these services It is done for free.

7.What is the purpose of holding international Olympiads by Erdos?

Today's world needs people who compete globally in different directions to get a decent and professional career. However, they must compete with experts from other countries and prove their knowledge for assignment in the desired job. This requires preparation from adolescence and familiarity with the academic level of students in other countries and to evaluate themselves. The Erdoş Institute has given students this exceptional opportunity by holding prestigious world championships.

The unique feature of participating in the International Olympiads of Erdos

The most obvious feature of the Olympiads&SASMO &VANDA- DRCT ,.... ; This is where the first stage of the competition is international, and unlike other common competitions, the results of the first round are compared with all the volunteers in the world and their rankings are announced globally from the official website of the competition.  A lot of knowledge. They mistakenly consider the word Olympiad to be equal to competition, but the truth is that participating in the Olympiad is not comparable to participating in the competition in terms of credibility and quality. Many international competitions are held only to acquaint students with global concepts and to communicate with students from other countries, and from a scientific point of view, it cannot be a strong resume to present to the scientific circles of the world. Many non-reputable institutions abused parents and students by using this misunderstanding of words.
