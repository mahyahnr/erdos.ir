vActivities of the Erdos Institute

1. What fields does Erdos Institute work from the beginning to the present?
 Erdoush started his career by holding classes of mathematics Olympiad preparation for volunteers to participate in the National Olympiad of Iran and during these years he has been able to provide brilliant activities in the fields of science, English and computer science. Among the following are summarized activities of Erdoş Institute:

  • Holding in-person and online classes across the country
  • Design and production of mathematical training equipment
  • Compilation and translation of educational books
  • Holding domestic and international competitions as the exclusive representative of the country throughout Iran
  • Holding domestic and international educational tours
  • Holding workshops and international conferences
  • Providing consulting and talent services (new)
For details of the above services, please visit our website at erdos.ir About Us.

2. What are the future activities of the Erdoush Institute?
 In the future, we will witness the unique and honorable services of Erdoush Institute in international fields because the needs of the world today require that every student of their age have an effective scientific relationship with their peers in other countries.


3. How do we find out about the activities of the Erdos Institute?
 You can follow Erdoush through the virtual space (Instagram, WhatsApp, Telegram) or by visiting our sites at erdosonline.ir or erdos.ir. Full contact information is given at the end of the brochure.
